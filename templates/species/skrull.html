Skrull Species

The Skrulls are a shape-shifting alien race in the Marvel Universe. Known for their incredible ability to mimic other beings, they have played significant roles in many galactic events.

Physical Characteristics

Skrulls typically have green skin and pronounced chins. They can alter their appearance to match virtually any form, including different species, genders, and ages.

Abilities

  • Shape-Shifting: Skrulls can perfectly mimic the appearance and abilities of other beings.
  • Genetic Memory: Skrulls inherit the genetic memory of their ancestors, enabling them to access the knowledge and skills of their lineage.
  • Telepathic: Some Skrulls possess telepathic abilities, allowing them to communicate with each other over long distances.

Species History

The Skrull race has a complex history of conflict, including wars with the Kree and their involvement in the Secret Invasion events on Earth.
